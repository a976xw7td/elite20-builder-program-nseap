# Teacher FAQ

## Q: How can I reuse this course?

Start from the weekly plan, select matching challenges, and use the teacher notes to prepare each session.

## Q: How should I evaluate AI-assisted work?

Evaluate the final output, the student's process, and the quality of reflection.

## Q: What if students use different AI tools?

Focus on learning outcomes and documented process instead of requiring one specific tool.

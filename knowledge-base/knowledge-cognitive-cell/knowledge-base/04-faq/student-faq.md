# Student FAQ

## Q: What should I do if I do not understand a challenge?

Start by reading the challenge goal, deliverables, and rubric. Then ask a Coding Coach agent to explain the task in smaller steps.

## Q: Can I use AI to complete assignments?

Yes, but you should show your process. The goal is not only to get an answer, but to learn how to work with AI responsibly.

## Q: What should I submit?

Submit the required deliverables listed in the challenge, plus a short reflection on how you used AI.

# AI-Assisted Learning Practice

## Purpose

Define a healthy pattern for using AI during learning.

## Practice

Use AI to clarify, compare, generate examples, review mistakes, and improve drafts. Do not use AI as a shortcut that hides your thinking.

## Reflection Questions

- What did AI help me understand?
- What did I still need to decide myself?
- What changed between my first attempt and final output?
- What would I ask differently next time?
